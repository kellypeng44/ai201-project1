CSE 373 - Analysis of Algorithms at Stony Brook University

Credits: 3
Class Size: 20-218 students
Average Professor Rating: 67
Usually Offered: TuTh (1 hour 20 minutes), MW (1 hour 20 minutes)

Description: Mathematical analysis of a variety of computer algorithms including searching, sorting, matrix multiplication, fast Fourier transform, and graph algorithms, covering complexity analysis and NP completeness, with programming components. Enrollment Requirements: Prerequisites: C or higher in CSE 113/150/215 or MAT 200/250; MAT 211 or AMS 210; CSE 214 or CSE 260; CSE/MAT/DAS major required.
