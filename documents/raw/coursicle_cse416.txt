CSE 416 - Software Engineering at Stony Brook University

Credits: 3
Class Size: 20-60 students
Average Professor Rating: 61
Usually Offered: TuTh (1 hour 20 minutes), MW (1 hour 20 minutes)
Attributes: ESI_PART, EXP+_PART, SBS+_PART, STEM+_PART

Description: This course introduces the basic concepts and modern tools and techniques of software engineering. It covers system requirements, specifications, object-oriented design, implementation, integration, testing, project management, documentation, maintenance, and human factors. Enrollment Requirements: Prerequisites: C or higher in CSE 316; U4 standing; CSE major.
