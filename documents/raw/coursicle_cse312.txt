Description
This course deals with the impact of computers on us as individuals and on our society. Rapid changes in computing technology and in our use of that technology have changed the ways we work, play, and interact with other people. These changes have created a flood of new social, legal and ethical issues that demand critical examination. This course is offered as both CSE 312 and ISE 312. Class Notes: Offered as CSE 312 and ISE 312. Enrollment Requirements: Prerequisites: CSE, ISE or DAS major; U3 or U4 standing; one D.E.C. E or SNW course.
